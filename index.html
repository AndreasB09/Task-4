Dangers lurk around every corner

How to survive from the shadows

Vampires

Hungry and ravenous. While sunlight is faster, fire is also most efficient. A stake through the heart will paralyze. Decapitate to finish them off permanently.

Werewolves

While the Garou tend to avoid civilization, you best keep your distance. The moonragers draw from the waxing and waning moon to make them into the most ferocius creatures on earth.

Mages

Being mere mortals, the Mages have their limits. Their witchcraft is powerful and only limited by their own mind. Exposing them can have paradoxal effects.

Demons

Escaped from the hells they posses mortal bodies. Slaying the host is but a temporary solution. To truly banish them one must undertake a complex ritual.

Changelings

Fae born into the mortal realm. These tricksters and shapeshifters follow their own code of honor. Easily affected by madness some can no longer differ between reality and dream. Cold Iron will cause grievous wounds even by the merest touch.

Hunters

These stalwart men and women are the defenders of humanity. Each one an exemplar of virtue yet thrust into a world not their own. Allies can be found within us.
